About J. R. Lawson

J.R. LAWSON is a self-published author of two novels so far for young adults. Among are, Forget Me Not—a lovable zombie romance, and Blood Land—a vampire adventure story. She says “As long as I’ve been enjoying making up stories (which is quite a long time) I’ve also enjoyed the spooky and supernatural. I’ve always been a fan of the classic movie monsters and love re-imagining them in unique ways and putting them in different situations. I have notebooks of ghost stories I used to write when I was in middle school that I go back to still from time to time.
